# Reel 141 - Read Support as a User-Health Dashboard

## Summary
Infrastructure health can remain green while users repeatedly encounter broken permissions, stale data, or incorrect business logic. Support tickets should be grouped by root cause and converted into UX fixes, missing telemetry, or engineering work.

## Verification Checklist
- Categorize tickets by root cause rather than reported symptom.
- Escalate repeated causes into product or engineering defects.
- Separate user-error, platform-error, and business-logic patterns.
- Add telemetry for user-visible failures monitoring currently misses.
- Review support patterns weekly with accountable follow-up actions.

## Skill Signal
Use support evidence to challenge green dashboards and find monitoring blind spots.
