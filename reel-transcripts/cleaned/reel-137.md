# Reel 137 - Shipping Is Not Go-to-Market

## Summary
A working product is not commercially launch-ready without a reachable customer segment, pricing tied to customer pain, and a concise problem-to-outcome message. Builder audiences are not automatically buyer audiences.

## Verification Checklist
- Identify where target customers already discuss the problem.
- Validate the problem and willingness to pay with real prospects.
- Price against measurable time, revenue, or risk saved.
- Express the product as one clear pain-to-resolution sentence.
- Separate vanity signups from qualified customer acquisition.

## Skill Signal
Flag missing customer, pricing, and positioning evidence separately from technical launch blockers.
