# Reel 101 - Open the Gates in Stages

## Summary
Canary release strategy is not only for infrastructure. The reel applies the same logic to product launch: let a controlled audience in first, watch the metrics, then widen access only after the system and behavior look healthy.

## Verification Checklist
- Use a limited early audience or traffic slice before treating a release as globally available.
- Watch activation, contribution, errors, and support signals during the controlled release window.
- Separate soft launch, canary release, and general availability in both technical and business operations.

Use this as a canary-launch scan for staged rollout discipline.
