# Reel 82 - Price for the World, Not One City

## Summary
A flat monthly price can become an adoption barrier across different economies. The reel recommends using purchasing power parity where appropriate and treating regional pricing as a growth architecture decision rather than a charity discount.

## Verification Checklist
- Check whether the current pricing model blocks legitimate users in major target regions.
- Evaluate purchasing power parity or regional pricing support in the billing stack.
- Treat regional pricing changes as part of growth strategy and product expansion, not only marketing copy.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a pricing scan for regional accessibility and expansion leverage.
