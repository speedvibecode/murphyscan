# Reel 24 - Rate Limiting Protects the Wallet

## Summary
Public APIs without limits can be abused by bots, scrapers, or brute-force attempts. The reel recommends rate limiting paid AI endpoints, authentication endpoints, and public data endpoints.

## Verification Checklist
- Add per-user and per-IP limits to expensive AI endpoints.
- Rate limit login, signup, password reset, and token endpoints.
- Cap public data endpoints to reduce scraping and uncontrolled traffic spikes.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a rate-limit scan for cost, abuse, and authentication protection.
