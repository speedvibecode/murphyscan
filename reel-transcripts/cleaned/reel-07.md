# Reel 07 - Demo to Product

## Summary
A demo can work with explanation; a product must work without it. The reel defines product readiness as stranger testing, deliberate breakage, and completion of unglamorous states like errors, loading, empty screens, resets, and legal pages.

## Verification Checklist
- Test the app with five target users who receive no explanation.
- Try hostile inputs: blanks, long strings, repeated submits, mobile layouts, and unexpected behavior.
- Confirm boring product states exist: loading, empty, error, password reset, terms, and basic support paths.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a productization scan that distinguishes working demos from shippable products.
