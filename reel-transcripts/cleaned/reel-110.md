# Reel 110 - Domain Requirements Change the Build

## Summary
The reel is partly a community announcement, but the reusable signal is that products in specific industries need domain-specific requirements. Education products, for example, need learning architecture, student-data privacy, parent/teacher controls, enrollment, and credentialing behavior that generic app scaffolds will not infer safely.

## Verification Checklist
- Identify whether the product sits in a regulated or domain-specific context such as education, healthcare, finance, government, real estate, or ecommerce.
- Verify the spec names domain obligations instead of relying on generic SaaS assumptions.
- For education products, check student-data privacy, role boundaries for parents/teachers/students, enrollment, credentialing, and institutional reporting needs.
- Treat domain requirements as launch scope when the product promise depends on them.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a domain-fit scan for industry-specific requirements that generic AI scaffolds are likely to miss.
