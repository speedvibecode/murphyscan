# Reel 142 - A Product Also Needs an Operating Business

## Summary
AI can accelerate software construction, but pricing, selling, onboarding, support, procurement, insurance, legal work, and ongoing operations remain separate capabilities. A technically ready product can still be commercially unready.

## Verification Checklist
- Identify who owns pricing, sales, onboarding, and support.
- Confirm customer-facing policies and contracts match actual operations.
- Check whether procurement and security questions have evidence-backed answers.
- Separate product launch readiness from company and go-to-market readiness.
- Do not treat working code as proof of a sustainable operating model.

## Skill Signal
Call out business-operating gaps when they materially affect launch, but keep them distinct from technical blockers.
