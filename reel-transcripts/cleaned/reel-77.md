# Reel 77 - Self-Hosted Auth Buys Control and Work

## Summary
BetterAuth represents the self-hosted side of the auth market, where teams keep user and session data in their own stack. The reel recommends evaluating whether the team truly wants that operational responsibility before choosing control over managed-provider speed.

## Verification Checklist
- Decide whether the team wants to own auth uptime, UI, migrations, and incident response.
- Compare self-hosted control against the launch speed and enterprise features of managed providers.
- Treat auth-provider choice as a sticky long-term decision, not a reversible experiment.
