# Reel 10 - Users Are Not Your QA Team

## Summary
Real users will test old devices, bad networks, unusual inputs, and confusing flows whether the builder planned for them or not. The reel recommends a small device matrix, aggressive edge-case input testing, and zero-context user testing.

## Verification Checklist
- Test on at least one weak device, small screen, old browser, or slow network.
- Enter apostrophes, emojis, empty fields, excessive text, and repeated submits.
- Observe target users using the app without coaching.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a QA coverage scan for real-world device and input behavior.
