# Reel 102 - Outgrowing Supabase Means Choosing a New Ceiling

## Summary
Once a bundled platform no longer fits, the next step is not “pick a replacement” in the abstract. The reel recommends comparing alternatives by scale-to-zero behavior, migration safety, read and write profiles, and the new database engine or ecosystem commitment they imply.

## Verification Checklist
- Compare alternatives by workload shape, cold-start tolerance, and migration ergonomics.
- Account for engine differences such as Postgres versus MySQL, and read-heavy edge storage versus write-heavy systems.
- Treat the replacement choice as a new architecture decision, not just a pricing reaction.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a post-platform scan for bundled-stack graduation decisions.
