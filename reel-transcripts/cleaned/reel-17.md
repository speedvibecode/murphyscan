# Reel 17 - Privacy and Terms Basics

## Summary
Apps that collect user data need basic legal and privacy coverage. The reel highlights privacy policy, terms of service, and CCPA/GDPR-style opt-out and deletion flows.

## Verification Checklist
- Confirm a privacy policy explains collected data, storage, use, and deletion.
- Verify terms of service cover liability, conduct, and dispute basics.
- Check for a user-facing data deletion or opt-out path when personal data is collected.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a compliance-basics scan for user-data apps.
