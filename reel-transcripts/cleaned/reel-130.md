# Reel 130 - First 48 Hours Decide Retention

## Summary
Launch success depends on whether new users reach value quickly after signup. The reel frames the first 48 hours as a measurable product-risk window: track whether users complete the core activation action, how quickly they reach the aha moment, and whether they return after day one.

## Verification Checklist
- Identify the one core action that predicts retained users.
- Track whether each new user completes that action within the first session or first 24 hours.
- Measure the path to the aha moment by steps, time, and percentage of signups reaching it.
- Flag users who do not return within 48 hours.
- Verify that nudges, emails, in-app messages, or tooltips exist for users who stall before activation.
- Treat weak activation or silent churn as a launch-readiness risk for products that depend on recurring use.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a product-activation scan for onboarding telemetry, aha-moment reach, churn signals, and first-48-hour follow-up loops.
