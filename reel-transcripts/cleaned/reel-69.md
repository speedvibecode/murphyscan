# Reel 69 - Pick the Serverless Database by Engine and Scale

## Summary
Neon and PlanetScale are both good options, but they optimize for different engines and team familiarity. The reel recommends choosing based on Postgres versus MySQL fit, safe branching for migrations, and production scale behavior rather than free-tier marketing.

## Verification Checklist
- Match the platform to the database engine your team already understands.
- Verify the schema branching and migration workflow before trusting production changes.
- Evaluate cost and operational behavior at meaningful scale instead of picking from prototype pricing alone.
