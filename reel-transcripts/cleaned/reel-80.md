# Reel 80 - Realtime Magic Versus Portable Boring

## Summary
Convex delivers a highly reactive developer experience, while Postgres delivers portability and decades of operational maturity. The reel recommends choosing based on whether the product truly needs Convex-style platform behavior and whether the team accepts the lock-in cost.

## Verification Checklist
- Confirm whether the product genuinely needs reactive realtime behavior by default.
- Evaluate lock-in risk for data model, query language, and migration path before adopting a platform database.
- Prefer portable relational infrastructure when product fit does not justify a specialized platform dependency.
