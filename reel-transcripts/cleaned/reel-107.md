# Reel 107 - Protect the Business Before Launch

## Summary
Launch risk extends past code. The reel calls out three checks many builders skip: cyber liability insurance for data-handling apps, platform terms that cap provider liability, and privacy policies that actually match collected data and deletion behavior.

## Verification Checklist
- Check whether the product handles user or customer data in a way that warrants cyber liability coverage or explicit owner risk acceptance.
- Review platform terms for liability caps, data-loss limits, and obligations that the product owner still carries.
- Compare the privacy policy against real data collection, payments, analytics, cookies, retention, and deletion behavior.
- Verify deletion/export paths exist when the policy or jurisdiction promises them.

Use this as a launch-liability scan for insurance readiness, platform-risk awareness, and policy-to-system fit.
