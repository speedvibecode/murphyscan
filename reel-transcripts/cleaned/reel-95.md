# Reel 95 - Privacy Pages Need Backing Systems

## Summary
Privacy risk lives in data collection paths, third-party analytics, and deletion gaps, not just in policy text. The reel recommends mapping all personal data flows, making consent meaningful, and building real deletion and retention behavior across databases, logs, backups, and vendors.

## Verification Checklist
- Inventory what personal data the system collects and where it is sent or stored.
- Ensure consent language and behavior match the actual third-party data movement.
- Build deletion and retention workflows that cover primary storage, analytics, logs, and backups where feasible.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a privacy-architecture scan for data flow honesty and deletion readiness.
