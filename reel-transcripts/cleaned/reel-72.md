# Reel 72 - Match the Auth Provider to the Customer

## Summary
Clerk and Auth0 are both strong, but they serve different business trajectories. The reel recommends Clerk for fast-moving modern SaaS teams and Auth0 for enterprise-heavy products that need SSO, directory integrations, and compliance expectations from day one.

## Verification Checklist
- Match auth-provider choice to target customers, not just developer ergonomics.
- Check whether the product roadmap requires SAML, LDAP, Active Directory, or enterprise buyer expectations.
- Avoid auth migrations by choosing with the expected business trajectory in mind.

## Skill Signal
Use this as an auth-provider scan for customer-fit and future migration risk.
