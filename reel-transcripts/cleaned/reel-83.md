# Reel 83 - SOC 2 Is a Trust Gate, Not a Late Add-On

## Summary
SOC 2 does not make a product better, but it often determines whether enterprise buyers will move forward at all. The reel recommends building the evidence trail early by treating access control, logging, incident response, and vendor management as long-lead operational systems.

## Verification Checklist
- Check whether target customers or pipeline deals require SOC 2 or equivalent trust signals.
- Verify the app has an evidence trail for access control, logging, incident handling, and vendor oversight.
- Avoid treating compliance as a late-stage paperwork exercise if the product is enterprise-bound.

## Skill Signal
Use this as an enterprise-readiness scan for trust-document preparation.
