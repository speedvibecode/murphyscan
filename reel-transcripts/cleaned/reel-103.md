# Reel 103 - AI Supply Chain Trust Is Now a Real Attack Surface

## Summary
AI-assisted development expands supply-chain risk beyond packages into prompts, downloaded skills, copied system instructions, and community resources. The reel recommends treating those inputs as trust-tiered artifacts, isolating unvetted materials from production, and rotating secrets immediately if contaminated inputs were used.

## Verification Checklist
- Separate first-party, vetted third-party, and unvetted community resources in the build and agent workflow.
- Prevent unreviewed prompts, skill files, or copied instructions from touching production-critical systems directly.
- Trigger secret rotation and review if a previously used external AI resource is later found unsafe or compromised.

## Skill Signal
Use this as an AI supply-chain scan for prompt, skill, and instruction trust boundaries.
