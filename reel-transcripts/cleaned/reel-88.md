# Reel 88 - Managed Buys Time, Self-Hosted Buys Responsibility

## Summary
Managed versus self-hosted infrastructure is mostly a time-versus-money trade, not a purity debate. The reel recommends starting with managed services when time is scarce, then moving only when economics or control requirements clearly justify owning the platform work.

## Verification Checklist
- Identify whether the team has spare operational capacity for patches, backups, and uptime ownership.
- Prefer managed services when infrastructure work would slow product delivery without clear benefit.
- Revisit self-hosting only when control or economics outweigh the ongoing operations burden.
