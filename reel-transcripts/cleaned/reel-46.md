# Reel 46 - Infrastructure Cost Discipline

## Summary
High infrastructure bills can erase product margins. The reel recommends auditing always-on resources, setting spend alerts, hard-capping where possible, and right-sizing databases based on measured usage.

## Verification Checklist
- Identify always-on resources that could scale to zero or downshift during idle periods.
- Configure budget alerts at multiple thresholds for every provider.
- Compare database plan capacity against actual memory, CPU, storage, and connection usage.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a cloud-cost scan for runaway spend and overprovisioned infrastructure.
