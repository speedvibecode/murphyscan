# Reel 31 - Availability and Recovery

## Summary
Production means the app can go down and still recover quickly. The reel defines a minimum recovery setup: automated backups, tested restores, uptime monitoring, and a one-page incident runbook.

## Verification Checklist
- Confirm automated database backups are enabled and retained.
- Test restore procedures instead of assuming backups are valid.
- Write a short runbook covering first checks, rollback, database status, and hosting logs.

## Skill Signal
Use this as a recovery-readiness scan.
