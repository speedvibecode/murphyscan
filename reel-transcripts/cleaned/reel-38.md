# Reel 38 - Reduce AI Cost Without Hurting UX

## Summary
AI costs can spike when every request goes to the most expensive model. The reel recommends semantic caching, complexity-based routing, batching, and debouncing to reduce spend while keeping output quality.

## Verification Checklist
- Cache semantically similar questions and reuse recent answers where safe.
- Classify requests by complexity and route to the cheapest adequate model.
- Debounce high-frequency interactions and batch document-style work.
