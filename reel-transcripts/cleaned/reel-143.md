# Reel 143 - Match Legal Documents to Product Reality

## Summary
Terms, privacy disclosures, refund rules, data-processing terms, enterprise service agreements, and risk transfer should reflect the product's actual data and operating behavior. Generic templates are not evidence of compliance or commercial readiness.

## Verification Checklist
- Verify terms define product-specific use and liability boundaries.
- Reconcile the privacy policy with actual collection, use, retention, and deletion.
- Check whether business customers require a DPA or MSA.
- Define refund handling before accepting payments.
- Ensure promised support, response, and uptime commitments are operable.
- Route cyber-liability coverage decisions to qualified owner or legal review.

## Skill Signal
Treat policy-to-system and contract-to-operations mismatches as launch risk; do not make legal conclusions from document presence alone.
